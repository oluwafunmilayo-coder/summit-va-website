Timmy Kay

Emerging Singer · Artist Operations Support

Timmy Kay is a talented new voice stepping into the music industry with passion and drive — but without the operational foundation to match. This case study walks through how Summit VA Agency would set him up for a sustainable, professional launch from day one.

This is a sample case study built to illustrate Summit VA's approach. It is not a record of completed work.

The Challenge

Starting out with talent — but no structure

Timmy is at the very beginning of his music career. He has the voice, the vision, and the hunger — but like most emerging artists, he's doing everything alone: managing his own emails, missing booking inquiries, posting inconsistently, and losing time to admin that should be going into his craft.

Without a proper operations layer, early opportunities get missed and momentum stalls before it even begins.

📬

No inbox management

Booking inquiries and collaboration DMs going unanswered or lost.

📅

No release calendar

Music drops without a proper rollout plan, content strategy, or promotion timeline.

💸

No financial tracking

No system for tracking gig payments, expenses, or earnings — just chaos.

🔗

No digital presence system

Bio links, EPK, and press assets scattered or non-existent.

Our Approach

Building the foundation behind the music

Summit VA steps in as Timmy's back-office operations partner — so he can stay focused on creating while we handle everything else.

1

Artist Onboarding & Setup

We start with a full operations audit — what tools he has, what's missing, and what needs to be built from scratch. We set up a professional Gmail, Google Drive structure, and a simple CRM to track contacts, venues, and opportunities.

2

Electronic Press Kit (EPK)

We build a clean, professional EPK with his bio, high-res photos, links to music, and booking contact info — the first thing any venue, playlist curator, or label will ask for.

3

Release Rollout Planning

For every single or project, we create a structured 4–6 week rollout calendar covering teaser content, release day, press outreach, and post-release engagement — so nothing is left to last-minute scrambling.

4

Inbox & Booking Coordination

We manage his professional inbox, respond to inquiries with templates we've crafted together, and keep a live tracker of all gig opportunities — confirmed, pending, and passed.

5

Financial Tracking & Admin

We set up a simple income and expense tracker, ensure gig contracts are filed properly, and send weekly financial summaries — so Timmy always knows where he stands.

6

Ongoing Executive Support

Monthly check-ins, task management, and priority planning to make sure as his career grows, the operations keep up with him — not the other way around.
